Core Principles & Habits
⚡
Work Extremely Hard
Nobody changes the world working 40 hours a week. Put in the hours when it matters — intensity beats consistency every time.
🧱
First Principles Thinking
Break every problem down to its fundamental truths. Don't reason by analogy — reason from the ground up, then rebuild your solution.
🔁
Rapid Feedback Loops
Constantly seek brutal, honest feedback. The faster you find out you're wrong, the faster you can be right. Criticism is data.
🗑️
Delete Before You Add
If you're not adding things back 10% of the time, you're not deleting enough. Simplicity is a superpower. Cut ruthlessly.
🚫
No Useless Meetings
Walk out of meetings that waste your time. Large meetings diffuse accountability. Small, focused, time-boxed — or don't meet at all.
🎯
Clear, Audacious Goals
Set a goal so big it seems insane. Work backwards. When the target is impossible, ordinary solutions stop making sense.
🧠
Hire Character Over Credentials
Hire for curiosity, drive, and honesty — not just qualifications. Exceptional people make exceptional companies.
😴
Protect Your Sleep
Six hours minimum. Sacrificing sleep backfires — poor decisions and slow thinking cost far more than the hours gained.
🌋
Embrace Failure as Data
If things are not failing, you are not innovating enough. Every failure is information. Collect it aggressively.
⚡ Your Daily Elon Challenge
1
Pick ONE thing you've been avoiding. Schedule it for today and do it first.
2
Find a process in your day that feels slow. Ask: what is the underlying constraint? Eliminate one step.
3
Ask one person for honest, critical feedback on something you're working on. Don't defend — just listen.
4
Identify a meeting or commitment this week that adds no real value. Cancel it or dramatically shorten it.
5
Write your most important goal in one sentence. Is everything you're doing today moving toward it?
